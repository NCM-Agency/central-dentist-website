## About Dr. Alejandrez

Dr. Elizabeth Alejandrez is dedicated to helping others improve their quality of life through biological dentistry. Her unique background in psychology and psychiatric rehabilitation, combined with her intuitive nature and curiosity, led her to expand her knowledge into biological dentistry to better serve her patients and implement the best practices for their overall well-being.

Dr. Alejandrez's collective experiences have shaped her into a considerate and compassionate dentist who centers her practice around creating a positive experience for her patients. She combines her psychology background with dental expertise to understand the whole person, not just their oral health.

She is focused on providing comprehensive care that addresses oral health and the whole body in an authentic and comfortable environment, making each patient feel heard and understood.

Outside of the clinic, Dr. Alejandrez enjoys traveling, challenging hikes, and having adventures with her husband and step-daughters. She loves playing sports and is currently embracing the discipline of tennis because, like dentistry, it is a constant and beautiful balance of learning, growth, and having fun!

This balance of professional dedication and personal fulfillment allows Dr. Alejandrez to bring fresh energy and genuine care to every patient interaction. Her psychology background helps her understand patient anxiety and create comfortable experiences for even the most nervous patients.

## Areas of Expertise

### Comprehensive Care
Comprehensive diagnosis and treatment planning for pediatric patients and adults, ensuring each treatment plan addresses the unique needs and goals of every patient.

### Restorative Solutions
Restorative solutions that address proper function and aesthetic goals including composite restorations, crowns, bridges, and removable prosthetics - all using biocompatible materials.

### Pediatric Dentistry
Specialized pediatric restorations, extractions, and evaluation of growth and sleep-disordered breathing. Creating positive dental experiences for children that last a lifetime.

### Invisalign & Orthodontics
Certified Invisalign provider addressing malocclusion and aesthetics while considering airway health and overall facial development.

### Airway Health
Comprehensive airway evaluation for signs and symptoms of sleep-disordered breathing, working collaboratively with sleep physicians and ENTs for optimal patient outcomes.
