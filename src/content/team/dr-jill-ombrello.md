## About Dr. Ombrello

Dr. Jill Rosellini Ombrello is the founder and lead dentist at Central Dentist. She was born and raised in North Dallas, attending Thomas Jefferson High School before pursuing her passion for dentistry.

Dr. Jill's journey into holistic dentistry began with a deep conviction that oral health is intimately connected to overall wellness. After experiencing firsthand the limitations of conventional dentistry, she dedicated herself to understanding how dental procedures and materials impact the entire body.

Her approach combines cutting-edge technology with time-tested biological principles. She believes in educating patients about the root causes of dental disease and empowering them to make informed decisions about their care. Whether performing a complex restorative procedure or a routine cleaning, Dr. Jill's focus is always on supporting the body's natural healing capacity.

## Areas of Expertise

### Mercury-Free & Metal-Free Dentistry
Specializing in the safe removal of mercury amalgam fillings and replacement with biocompatible materials. Dr. Jill is SMART-certified for safe mercury removal protocols.

### Ceramic Implants
Expert in metal-free, biocompatible ceramic implant placement and restoration, offering patients a holistic alternative to traditional titanium implants.

### Sleep & Airway Therapy
Comprehensive evaluation and treatment of sleep-disordered breathing, including sleep apnea, UARS, and pediatric airway issues using orthodontic, orthopedic, and myofunctional approaches.

### Biomimetic Restorative Dentistry
Advanced training in biomimetic techniques that preserve tooth structure and mimic nature's design for longer-lasting, more conservative restorations.

### TMJ & Orofacial Pain
Holistic treatment of temporomandibular joint disorders and chronic orofacial pain using orthopedic appliances, myofunctional therapy, and craniosacral techniques.

### Pediatric Airway & Development
Early intervention for optimal craniofacial development, proper airway formation, and prevention of orthodontic problems in children.
