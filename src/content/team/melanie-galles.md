## About Melanie

Melanie Galles is a dedicated biological hygienist with a genuine concern for the importance of dental health. Her extensive education and certifications in holistic dentistry, combined with her compassionate approach, make her an invaluable member of our team committed to helping patients achieve optimal oral and overall health.

Melanie's commitment to dental health extends far beyond the office. She has been involved with numerous charitable organizations and dental outreach programs, demonstrating her genuine desire to help others who are less fortunate.

Outside of her professional life, Melanie enjoys an active lifestyle that includes traveling, working out, church activities, and cooking. She loves sports, riding horses, hiking, and fishing. This balance of professional dedication and personal fulfillment allows her to bring fresh energy and genuine care to every patient interaction.

## Areas of Expertise

### Biological Dental Hygiene
Providing comprehensive hygiene services using only biocompatible products and techniques that support the body's natural healing processes and overall wellness.

### Nutritional Counseling
Educating patients on the vital connection between nutrition and oral health, providing personalized dietary guidance to support dental wellness and systemic health.

### Ozone Therapy
Utilizing medical-grade ozone for natural antimicrobial treatment, promoting healing and eliminating harmful bacteria without the use of harsh chemicals.

### Laser Dentistry
Certified in advanced laser techniques for gentle, minimally invasive periodontal therapy and bacterial reduction with enhanced patient comfort.
